Cover illustration. A high-schooler's #2 pencil rendering of a generic
suburban house on horizontal-ruled notebook paper — vinyl siding, dark
green shutters, attached garage, a Dodge Caravan in the driveway with a
TMBG Flood sticker, basketball hoop slightly crooked. A figure in
shorts, t-shirt, and baseball cap mows the too-perfect sitcom lawn.
A telephone pole with lost-dog and yard-sale flyers occupies the left
margin. Telephone wires droop in catenary curves across the upper
composition. Selective ballpoint pen color fills the shutters, lawn,
tree canopy, and roof over the pencil structure. DON'T PROMPT is rubber-
stamped in red over the lawn, askew. Helvetica Black title and subtitle
top-aligned to the red margin line. Author name and CC BY-SA 4.0
handwritten in blue ballpoint on the ruled lines at the bottom, centered.
