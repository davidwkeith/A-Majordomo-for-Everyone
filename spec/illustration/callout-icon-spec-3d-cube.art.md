Callout Icon: The isometric 3D cube. Three visible faces drawn in perspective — top, left, right — the doodle every kid drew when they learned that two diagonal lines and a few parallels make a box look real. Precise parallel lines, consistent angles, maybe a faint vanishing-point construction line visible beneath. The top face slightly lighter pressure, the right face shaded with light crosshatching to give it depth. Technical, structural, a shape defined by its specification. No color — just graphite. Margin-sized, roughly 1cm tall. The same hand that drew the Trinitron.

**Background: pure white, `#FFFFFF`, flat.** Not gray, not off-white, not cream, not paper texture, not a card, not a notebook page. Just the pencil drawing on a clean white field. The build removes white to create transparency, so any gray will show as a halo in the ePub.
