Callout Icon: The Cool S. Six vertical lines (three left, three right) connected by diagonal strokes at top and bottom into a pointed S shape. Drawn as a kid would draw it in a notebook margin — construction lines faintly visible beneath the finished form. The S is symmetrical and careful, as if drawn early in the school year when it still took concentration. No color, no shading — just graphite. Margin-sized, roughly 1cm tall. The same hand that drew the Trinitron.
