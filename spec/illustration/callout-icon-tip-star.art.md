Callout Icon: A five-pointed star drawn in one continuous stroke — the intersecting-lines method, not outlined and filled. Drawn slowly and carefully, the angles nearly even, the star close to regular. You can see where the pencil started and where it returned to close the shape — a slight overlap at the starting point. The gold star. The thing that marks something worth remembering. No color, no shading — just graphite. Margin-sized, roughly 1cm tall. The same hand that drew the Trinitron.

**Background: pure white, `#FFFFFF`, flat.** Not gray, not off-white, not cream, not paper texture, not a card, not a notebook page. Just the pencil drawing on a clean white field. The build removes white to create transparency, so any gray will show as a halo in the ePub.
