#2 pencil sketch of an original iPad. On the screen 8-bit pixel art of Roy and Moss from The IT Crowd, hand drawn with pen. Roy holds a phone with visible frustration. Moss holds a fire extinguisher. Both are approximately 32x48 pixels scaled up. Colored in ballpoint pen over #2 pencil grid on white background. 4-color palette per character. The iPad frame is sketched in pencil around the pixel art — the bezel is thick and unmistakable, the home button a perfect circle. The whole thing looks like a kid drew their favorite show on the exciting new device, which is exactly what happened.
