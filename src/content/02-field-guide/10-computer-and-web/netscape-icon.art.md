The original Netscape Navigator ship's wheel icon, rendered in 8-bit pen pixel art as it appeared on a Mac System 7 desktop. 32x32 pixels scaled up. The icon sits on a tiny white square with a drop shadow — the System 7 icon frame. Colored in ballpoint pen: blue ocean, brown ship's wheel, white N. The whole thing is drawn on a plain white background in #2 pencil with ballpoint color fill. This is what the internet looked like before it became five apps — a single icon on a desktop, promising everything.
