Inline Graphic 8-bit Pixel Art: The classic Slashdot topic icon: Bill Gates as a Borg drone. 32x32 pixels scaled up. Gates's face with glasses, assimilated — a Borg eyepiece over one lens, metallic implants on the temple and jaw. The expression is serene in the way only the assimilated can be. Colored in ballpoint pen over #2 pencil grid on plain white background. 4-color palette: skin tone, metallic gray, glasses black, Borg green LED. This was how Slashdot tagged every story about Microsoft in the late '90s. The platforms changed. The assimilation didn't.
