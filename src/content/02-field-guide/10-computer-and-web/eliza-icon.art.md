A Mac System 7 app icon for ELIZA — the 1966 MIT chatbot, the original AI conversation. 32x32 pixels scaled up. The icon: a speech bubble containing a question mark, rendered in the flat, 1-bit style of early Mac icons. Sits on the standard System 7 white icon square with drop shadow. Below it, in Chicago font (hand-lettered in pencil), the label "ELIZA." Drawn in #2 pencil on plain white background, no color. The first AI agent had no intelligence and no answers. It just asked good questions. Sixty years later, that is still the skill.
