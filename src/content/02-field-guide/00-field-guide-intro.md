# PART TWO: THE FIELD GUIDE

*Short entries organized by life domain. Each entry opens with an Adams-format description of the situation, followed by the strategy tag, the confirmed spec, and what to do with the output. Entries are not exhaustive -- they are representative. The situations chosen are the ones most people will actually encounter.*

*The entry format:*
```
## [Entry Title]
**Strategy:** [which of the nine strategies this uses]
**The Majordomo says:** [Adams-format opener, 2-3 sentences]
**The spec:** [the confirmed prompt, ready to use or lightly adapt]
**What to do with the output:** [one paragraph]
**Science note:** [one sentence + footnote, where applicable]
```
