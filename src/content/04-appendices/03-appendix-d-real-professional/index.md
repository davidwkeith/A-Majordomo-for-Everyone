## Appendix D: When You Need a Real Professional

Claude is the beginning of the process, not the end, for any situation involving:

- A signed legal document with financial consequences over $1,000
- A medical diagnosis requiring treatment decisions
- A tax situation involving back taxes, audits, or business income
- A workers' compensation or personal injury claim
- Any situation where someone has threatened to sue you or been sued by you
- Any immigration matter with enforcement consequences

**Finding affordable professional help:**

*Legal*
- **lawhelp.org** -- free or reduced-cost legal services by state
- **immigrationadvocates.org** -- immigration legal services directory
- **aclu.org** -- civil rights and discrimination complaints
- **nhlp.org** -- National Housing Law Project, tenant rights resources

*Financial*
- **nfcc.org** -- National Foundation for Credit Counseling, nonprofit
- **benefits.gov** -- federal benefits eligibility screening
- **aceee.org/consumers** -- energy efficiency rebates and programs by state

*Health*
- **988lifeline.org** -- crisis support
- **psychologytoday.com/us** -- therapist finder with filter for sliding-scale fees
- **hrsa.gov/find-health-care** -- federally qualified health centers, income-based fees
- **needymeds.org** -- prescription assistance programs

*Education*
- **nasfaa.org** -- financial aid guidance for students and families
- **firstgenfound.org** -- resources for first-generation college students

*Environmental and Energy*
- **energysaver.gov** -- federal efficiency programs and rebates
- **dsireusa.org** -- state-by-state incentive database for renewable energy
- **benefits.gov/weatherization** -- weatherization assistance for low-income households

### The Line

The AI brings the library. A professional brings the library *and* the life — the years of practice, the pattern recognition from a thousand similar cases, the judgment that comes from having been wrong before and knowing what it cost. A doctor has read the same studies the AI has read. The doctor has also watched patients respond to treatments in ways the studies did not predict. A lawyer has read the same statutes. The lawyer has also stood in front of the specific judge who will hear your case and knows how that judge thinks.

The AI is an incredibly well-read person who was born this morning. A professional is a well-read person with scar tissue. For the 95% of situations in this book — understanding a document, preparing for a conversation, drafting a letter, mapping your options — the library is enough. For the situations listed above, you need someone whose knowledge has been tested against reality, someone who carries professional liability for the advice they give, and someone who will still be accountable next year if the advice was wrong.

Use the AI to understand your situation. Use the professional to act on it. The AI gets you from confusion to clarity for free. The professional gets you from clarity to outcome with accountability. Both are part of the infrastructure the billionaire class has always had. Now you have the first half for free and a directory for the second.

*A note on this appendix:* These organizations exist because the systems they navigate are genuinely complex and the stakes are genuinely high. Your Agent can help you understand your situation and prepare for the conversation. These organizations help you act on it with the support of someone who carries professional accountability. Use both.
