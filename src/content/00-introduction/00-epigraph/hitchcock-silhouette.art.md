Hitchcock's famous profile silhouette — the unmistakable
round belly, double chin, and pursed lower lip in side view.
Rendered as a solid fill in #2 pencil, heavily shaded to near-black.
Not a detailed portrait — the silhouette only, the same one he
drew in single lines on his TV show. Pure white background.
Small, approximately the height of three lines of text.
