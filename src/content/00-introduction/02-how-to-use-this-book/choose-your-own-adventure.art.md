A Choose Your Own Adventure paperback drawn in #2 pencil with
the Trinitron level of obsessive detail. The book is slightly
worn — creased spine, dog-eared corners, the cover art suggested
but not rendered in detail. The reader's hand holds the book
open with one thumb on the current page. The page text is suggested in tiny
illegible pencil scribbles, but the "Turn to page 47" at the
bottom is legible. Transparent background. Pencil only, no
ballpoint — this is a domestic object, not pixel art.
