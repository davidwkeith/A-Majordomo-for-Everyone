TRINITRON CHAPTER OPENER. Al behind the shoe store counter,
pointing at nothing in particular, one shoe on the counter. A
customer in the chair, expression unreadable. Al's face: the
specific look of a man who is right and knows it and cannot
leverage that rightness into an outcome. Paper angle: determined
by Al's pointing gesture — horizontal right, dead level or almost.
The blue lines extend Al's point across the frame and off the
edge: the right that goes nowhere, extended to infinity. Obsession
zone: the Sony logo on the front bezel, drawn letter by letter
with the precision of someone who has traced corporate lettering
a hundred times — the specific proportions of each letterform,
the slight optical adjustments, the exact weight.
Caption: >> AL: I know my rights.
[He does not know his rights.]
