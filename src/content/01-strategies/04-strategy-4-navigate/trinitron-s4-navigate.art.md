TRINITRON CHAPTER OPENER. Jerry and Kramer side by side in a
hospital observation gallery, both leaning over the railing with
expressions of dawning horror. The surgeon below looks up at them.
The scene viewed from slightly above and behind so their faces are
visible in profile. Paper angle: determined by the downward lean
over the railing — steep, roughly 40 degrees, vertiginous. Blue
lines plunge downward toward the operating table, extending the
trajectory of the Junior Mint. Obsession zone: the screen glass
surface itself — the faint fingerprints on the glass, the barely
visible reflection of the room in the curved surface, the anti-glare
coating texture rendered in obsessive stippling. The place where you
can see the glass is glass, not just a window into the image. The
surface between you and the picture.
